White Turf Protection Film

White Turf Protection Film is a great one-way vision film that delivers privacy during the day looking in and a clear view looking out. This is because your eye can look past the black lining and focus just on the visible light coming through. Very Similar to looking out a window screen.

This product is designed to help protect your artificial turf and natural grass by reducing the sun’s harmful UV rays/reflection from your residential or commercial windows.

Ice Blue Turf Protection Film

Ice blue Turf Protection film is a fantastic exterior film that provides additional benefits while protecting your turf from burning. The ultimate goal is to minimize the sun’s harmful reflection from your windows to your turf. Ice blue turf protection film has been shown to help prevent turf burn by decreasing reflective glare up to 16%.

With the melting point of 175-200 degrees for most artificial turf this film can reduce up to 125 degrees once applied.

Ice blue film has a subtle blue tint and is accompanied with 63% heat rejection properties plus 99.9 % UV protection.
