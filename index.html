Turf Protection Film

Turf Guard screen film is perforated so it is similar to looking in and out of a bug screen. The screen film is also applied to the exterior, however it provides visibility both in and out of the window. The film is offered in clear, white, black, and silver, which is the most common.

Adding turf guard window film to your windows that face your turf will prevent burning and discoloration. Without the window film, the reflection from energy efficient windows can magnify the sun, and cause your turf to melt and burn
